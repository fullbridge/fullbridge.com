Global Partners

Fullbridge believes in partnerships. Lyra technology empowers learning that is scalable and delivered in partnership with a network of coaches, institutions, and governments.

Fullbridge Coaches

People learn from people. Lyra Learning Labs and Working Labs can be facilitated by in-house leadership with our training and preparation, or by one of our specialist coaches. Fullbridge coaches are world-class leaders who model professional behavior, share their experiences and feedback, and help participants navigate the challenges they encounter in learning and working labs.

Aptima, Inc.

Aptima, Inc. has conducted over $200 million in R&D on competency learning and measurement for NASA and organizations within the U.S. military and healthcare industry. Fullbridge collaborates with Aptima and its more than 60 specialists in I/O psychology and cognitive science on the development of tools and methodologies.

The College of the Holy Cross

The College of the Holy Cross is consistently ranked among leading four-year liberal arts colleges in the U.S. It designed a business certificate program that helps students get real-world work experience. The certificate program kicks off with a week-long immersive, project-based program designed and run by Fullbridge.

Prince Sultan University

Prince Sultan University (PSU) is the first private university in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Fullbridge and PSU work together to develop and conduct immersive four-week programs that give its Saudi students their first real work experience and prepare them for successful and productive integration into their internships.

Rwanda Management Institute

The Rwanda Management Institute (RMI) is a national government institution that builds professional capacities in Rwandan organizations in the public and private sectors. RMI has partnered with Fullbridge to deliver programming that helps participants develop and refine their leadership and management capacities.
