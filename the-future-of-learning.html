The Future of Learning

Training Is Dead

Trainings on everything from communication to coding quickly become obsolete when new programs and data are released. To stay ahead of the curve of change, organizations must create learning cultures focused on agility, business objective alignment, and new ways of meeting daily challenges. Best practices quickly become outdated. Online videos don’t build real capacity and knowledge. Our world of constant change has buried traditional training.

Learning on the Curve of Change

Lyra has revolutionized learning on the curve of change by bringing innovative measurement and data to experiential learning. Lyra combines business concepts with real-world experiences and enables people to engage in teams as they develop and practice measurable competencies. With Lyra, organizations can increase their idea flow, problem-solving, and daily innovation. It’s fun, eye-opening, impactful, and transformative.

Lyra’s Unbiased View Drives Success.

Because Lyra continues to look at what matters most (without the normal biases we all have as humans), Lyra is able to pinpoint areas of improvement in individuals that will best help their team reach clear objectives.
