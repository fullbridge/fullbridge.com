Lyra™

Fullbridge learning experiences are powered by our proprietary Lyra Technology. Lyra is the first and only learning system to incorporate a new science of experiential learning—a unique high-tech, high-touch approach that puts individuals and teams into measurable problem solving experiences to find and fill competency gaps.

Data-Driven Experiential Learning

Lyra is a knowledge technology hub that captures data from wearable devices, peer, self, and expert assessments, and creates personalized learning from an ever-expanding pool of educational content.

Lyra processes multiple layers of data on underlying competencies to give participants a rich picture of their performance and a better understanding of how to develop the self-awareness, knowledge, skills, and strategies that drive daily problem-solving and innovation.

The Lyra SkillsPrint

Lyra translates data it collects into a SkillsPrint—giving participants specific and actionable feedback on how their knowledge and behaviors contribute to, or detract from, their ability to effectively contribute to problem-solving. Lyra helps evaluate key areas of performance including business knowledge, concepts, and capacities such as planning, teamwork, and communications. Lyra successfully guides individuals to the learning that will have the greatest impact on improving the skills they need most to successfully contribute to their teams.

Lyra Management Insights

Management is constantly trying to understand why some teams or divisions succeed, while others struggle or fail. Lyra helps provide the key insights management needs to better understand what skills, capacities and behaviors drive success in their fields and then how to build those capacities that matter.

Accessibility

When providing learning programs for employees, organizations must be mindful of federal regulations governing accessibility according to the guidelines of the Americans with Disabilities Act. Fullbridge in conformance with Level AA of the W3C Web Content Accessibility Guidelines 2.0 throughout our learning experiences, making our learning labs and modules a safe way for our partners to ensure that all employees are included and respected.
