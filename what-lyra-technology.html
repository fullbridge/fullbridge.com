Lyra™

Fullbridge learning experiences are powered by our proprietary Lyra Technology. Lyra is the first and only learning system to incorporate a new science of experiential learning—a unique high-tech, high-touch approach that puts individuals and teams into measurable problem solving experiences to find competency gaps.

Data-Driven Experiential Learning

Lyra is a knowledge technology hub that captures data from wearable devices, peer, self, and expert assessments, and creates personalized learning from an ever-expanding pool of educational content.

Lyra processes multiple layers of data on underlying competencies to give participants a rich picture of their performance and a better understanding of how to develop the self-awareness, knowledge, skills, and strategies that drive daily problem-solving and innovation.

The Lyra SkillsPrint

Individual skills information is translated into a Lyra SkillsPrint—giving participants specific and actionable feedback on how their knowledge and behaviors contribute to, or detract from, their ability to contribute effectively to problem solving. Lyra helps test key areas of performance including; business knowledge, concepts, and capacities such as planning, teamwork, and communications. Lyra successfully guides individuals to the learning that will have the greatest impact on improving the skills they need most to successfully contribute to their teams.

Lyra Management Insights

Management is constantly trying to understand why some teams or divisions succeed, while others struggle or fail. Lyra helps provide the key insights management needs to better understand what skills, capacities and behaviors drive success in their fields and then how to build those capacities that matter.
